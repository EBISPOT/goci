 Anniversary of GWAS Catalog

The GWAS Catalog was launched in 2008 to provide a publicly available central repository of all published GWAS and association results. Over the last 10 years the Catalog has grown, from containing the first published GWAS on age-related macular degeneration, into the primary source of disease related associations. The Catalog now contains over #, with global reach and over # visitors per week. This vast amount of data fuels research into common disease, enabling investigations to identify causal variants, understand disease mechanisms, and establish targets for novel therapies.

Structured ancestry information added for all studies, recently published.

Targeted arrays, focusing on exonic regions of the genome or candidate loci. Collaboration with Open Targets to expand scope to include studies employing these.
